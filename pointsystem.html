Point System Rules
You will be given popsicle sticks! Each cabin will be given their color. The goal is to create as many hashtags and place on their flag. The purpose of putting these hashtags on the flag is to make it as stiff as possible. Nobody likes to look at a limping flag.
The hashtags must be made of whole popsicle sticks, no breaking them! Each hashtag must be made of 4 popsicle sticks.
The flagpole itself will be chopped down if a team has poor sportsmanship, or any other misbehavior that is not consistent with the camp spirit. The recreational team takes the spirit of camp competition seriously.
Popsicle sticks can be collected through winning games, memory verses, # of fishes caught, archery, big swing, good sportsmanship, cabin tidiness, cafeteria duties, flagpole themes, and few other methods
